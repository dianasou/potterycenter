About: Pottery Center

Here at Pottery Center, we welcome people with all levels of artistic talent! We just ask for enthusiasm and willingness to learn. For people with more advanced knowledge of ceramics and pottery, we have advanced workshops and open studio hours to allow creative freedom.


Pottery Center started as a community for people who love creating and art. Allowing people to show their artistic expression provides many benefits to individuals. It can relieve stress or anxiety. Being around people who enjoy similar things also provides a community for many.

Call us for any speical events with big parties. We would love to host you!

Hours: Mon-Fri 9 am - 10 pm, Sat 9 am - 5 pm
